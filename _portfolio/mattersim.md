MatterSim is a unified deep learning atomistic model for precise and generalizable in silico materials characterization. During my Microsoft Research internship, I contributed benchmarks and computational workflows for model design and training data generation.

My work involved high-throughput datasets, inference on large models, Azure Cloud pipelines, and tools such as VASP, atomate2, joblib, phonopy, phono3py, ESEN, MACE, and ORB.
