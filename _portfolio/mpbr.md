MPBR explores open-set fine-grained recognition in a more open-world setting. The project uses multimodal progressive bidirectional reasoning to suppress semantic drift and improve recognition under unknown distributions.

I developed benchmark pipelines and evaluated leading OSR models on iNat2021-OSR, using tools such as t-SNE, Grad-CAM, Swin Transformer, and HAN-OSFGR.
