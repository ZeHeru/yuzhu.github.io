This project developed a SERS-based imaging approach for fingerprint recognition and chemical mapping of latent and live fingerprints under varied environmental conditions.

I collected fingerprint samples, performed SERS imaging, and analyzed results for non-invasive chemical identification of residues such as drugs or nicotine.
